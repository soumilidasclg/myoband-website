SYNERKSIS
Making Human Motion Digital
SCAN TO ACCESS

PROBLEM

  • Athletes & patients lack real-time muscle visibility.
  • Existing systems are expensive, bulky, & lab-bound.
  • Data is complex and hard to interpret.

SOLUTION

  • MyoBand: A compact wearable for any setting.
  • Measures EMG (Muscle) + IMU (Motion).
  • Generates clear, AI-based performance reports.

WHAT WE MEASURE

  • Peak Muscle Activation & Timing.
  • Fatigue Index & Endurance.
  • Left–Right Muscle Balance.

IMPACT

  • Sports Performance Analysis.
  • Injury Prevention.
  • Rehabilitation Tracking.
  • Biofeedback-based Training.
